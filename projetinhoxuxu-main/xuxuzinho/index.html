Happy birthday my Darling


My dear Warren, kind and adorable, who makes me feel the same incredible feeling I felt since the day we kissed for the first time — even though I have some doubts about whether it was in the library or the park. But I know for sure that my love for you keeps growing every day. Every day I discover more about you, I want even more to stay by your side forever. You have changed me so deeply that the Mariana from two years ago wouldn’t recognize me. I am very happy with this change because you made me love intensely, made me feel an indescribable love. But love itself is so complex, and we have only one word to describe it: love. The Greeks had nine words to distinguish love, each with a different meaning, yet none is as complex as the love I feel for you. Some philosophers have a peculiar description of love, but for me, love is about staying even after passion fades, seeing the imperfections and choosing to stay. Love, above all, is a kind of diplomacy; it must be reciprocal, because both must love, but they love in different ways. I think that is the final touch: the difference between the two and the way of dealing with conflicts — not necessarily fights, but situations — together, and getting to know each other more and more. Love is staying together and loving that act. I love you with all my strength, I love the person you are, the person you are becoming, and the person you were. Even though we remain in the same flesh, we are always constantly changing, and I see that change in you. In this one year and almost three months, I have seen you go through so much, I saw how you handled it, learned, and evolved. And I cannot stop loving you, my love. I love you so much, I am so passionate about everything you do, did, and will do. The way you solve things makes me want to burst with so much passion for you. There are no words capable of expressing this — neither the Greeks, nor the philosophers, nor myself can describe it. But I know that on the day I return to Canada and look into your eyes, you will see this love overflowing in tears when you see me. I am counting the days to see you, my love. I want to live every day of my life with you. It doesn’t matter if we fight or argue, I know we will solve all of life’s problems together, because we are in this together, my love. I love my little darling so much. Happy birthday! You are growing so fast, and time is passing quickly too. Soon, we will be together, and I will be able to kiss you and look into your beautiful face every day.



Do you love me?
